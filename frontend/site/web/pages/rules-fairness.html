20 Verification Rules

⚖️
Rule 1
Equal Treatment Verification
Ensures individuals or groups in similar situations are treated equally without arbitrary distinctions.
⚖️
Rule 2
Non-Discrimination Principle
Verifies content does not discriminate based on protected characteristics or membership in vulnerable groups.
⚖️
Rule 3
Equitable Access Assessment
Checks that information or opportunities are equitably accessible to all relevant populations.
⚖️
Rule 4
Representation Fairness
Ensures all voices and perspectives are fairly represented in examples and case studies.
⚖️
Rule 5
Due Process Assurance
Verifies fair procedures are in place for decision-making and conflict resolution.
⚖️
Rule 6
Transparency in Rules
Ensures criteria for decisions are transparent and clearly communicated to affected parties.
⚖️
Rule 7
Reasonableness Evaluation
Checks that policies, rules, or constraints are reasonable and justified.
⚖️
Rule 8
Proportionality Assessment
Verifies consequences are proportional to the situation and not unnecessarily harsh.
⚖️
Rule 9
Appeal Rights Validation
Ensures individuals have meaningful opportunity to challenge unfair decisions.
⚖️
Rule 10
Consistency in Application
Verifies rules and standards are applied consistently across similar situations.
⚖️
Rule 11
Burden Distribution Fairness
Checks that burdens, costs, or responsibilities are fairly distributed across society.
⚖️
Rule 12
Benefit Distribution Equity
Ensures benefits and opportunities are equitably distributed without unfair advantage.
⚖️
Rule 13
Counterbalance Check
Verifies that advantages for some groups don't come at unreasonable disadvantage to others.
⚖️
Rule 14
Merit-Based Assessment
Ensures decisions are based on relevant merit and qualifications, not irrelevant factors.
⚖️
Rule 15
Context Sensitivity
Considers relevant contextual factors that affect what's fair in specific situations.
⚖️
Rule 16
Reciprocity Principle
Verifies mutual obligations and reciprocal treatment across relationships.
⚖️
Rule 17
Narrative Fairness
Ensures narratives and stories represent situations fairly and comprehensively.
⚖️
Rule 18
Vulnerability Protection
Ensures vulnerable populations receive appropriate protections and consideration.
⚖️
Rule 19
Remedy Adequacy
Verifies that remedies for unfair treatment are adequate and effective.
⚖️
Rule 20
Systemic Fairness
Evaluates fairness at the system level, not just individual transactions.
